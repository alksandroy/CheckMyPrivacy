Services

CheckMyPrivacy offers comprehensive consulting, partnership, and integration services to ensure optimal privacy compliance and protection for individuals and businesses alike.

Consulting Services

We offer personalized consulting services for businesses that need help understanding or improving their privacy policies. This could include help with compliance with various regulations including GDPR, CCPA, etc.

1. Comprehensive Privacy Strategy Development: Assist businesses in developing a comprehensive privacy strategy that aligns with their business model and customer expectations. This includes not just compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA but also adopting privacy as a competitive advantage in the marketplace.

2. Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA): Offer services to conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments for new and existing projects or technologies that process personal data. This helps in identifying and mitigating risks to privacy at an early stage.

3. Privacy by Design Consultation: Provide consultation on integrating Privacy by Design principles into product development processes. This involves advising on technical and organizational measures to ensure that privacy is considered at all stages of product development.
