About Us

From the CheckMyPrivacy Team.

Who Are We?

We are a passionate team of college students who understand the need of protecting personal information in today's digital age. To address this requirement, we are currently developing an innovative privacy policy checker capable of scanning any website or privacy policy submitted to it.

As college students ourselves, we understand the significance of digital privacy in academic, professional, and personal spheres. Whether it's conducting research, communicating with peers, or engaging in online activities, we believe everyone deserves peace of mind knowing where their information is going and how it is being used.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a web experience where users can understand how their information is being used and know that their privacy is being protected.

The Importance of Privacy

In today's digital age, information and data are everything. Your personal information could be scattered across the internet the moment you enter it into a website. At CheckMyPrivacy, we're here to help you protect your information and maintain your privacy.

How does our Privacy Decoder work?

Our Privacy Decoder leverages the ChatGPT API to analyze online data, extracting and deciphering privacy policies from various websites. It summarizes the findings, assigns them a clarity rating, and offers users insightful guidance on how to navigate data provision on each specific site.
